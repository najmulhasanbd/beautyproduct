Niacinamide, also known as vitamin B3, is a water-soluble vitamin that plays a crucial role in maintaining healthy skin. It is known for its anti-inflammatory properties and ability to improve the skin's barrier function. Niacinamide can help reduce redness, minimize pores, and improve overall skin texture. It is commonly used in skincare products to address issues such as acne, hyperpigmentation, and signs of aging. Additionally, niacinamide has antioxidant properties that help protect the skin from environmental damage caused by free radicals.
